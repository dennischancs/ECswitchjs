# Vocab001 ## 2013年text3
  1. belt (1) (物体)带：a sword-belt 配剑带，safety belt安全带；a conveyor belt 传送带；
  2. belt (2) (地理)地带，区域：green belts绿化带, a pine belt, a forest belt; belt highway = beltway 环城高速。
  3. belt (3) 系带子：Your mackintosh/raincoat looks better belted. 雨衣系上带子更好看。
  4. below the belt unfairly
  5. belt and braces 【喻】双重保险, (政策)稳妥可靠
  6. hold the belt（在拳击、摔交等运动中）保持冠军位置（保主了金腰带）
  7. a thrifty housekeeper 节俭的主妇
  8. a thrifty farm 富庶的农庄（原因：节俭、勤劳的农庄才富庶）
